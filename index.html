    OPPORTUNITIES

    COMMERCIAL OPPORTUNITIES

    The Angelwing Hospitality Compass (AHC) provides consultancy services to businesses within the hospitality sector, as well as providing services to upmarket contract caterers and private dining and corporate hospitality events. We also project manage and run new restaurants in association with hoteliers and entrepreneurs.

    Our companies are not clones of one another. Each one has its own identity and characteristics. There's no set template. We work with interior designers and architects restaurant by restaurant, bar by bar. There's no single ownership structure. Sometimes, we set up in partnership with hoteliers and other businesses, sometimes on our own.

    COMMERCIAL PARTNERSHIPS

    The model is a joint-venture project; there's a synergy between us and our partners and a shared commitment to creating something great - with its own unique identity, recognizable by its values rather than its corporate brand. When we help a hotelier or other business open a venue, we 'second' a team of key personnel to the project. Importantly, our General of Operations cum Executive Chef Consultant takes a direct role in quality control - we're in the project for the long haul. For commercial project enquiries, please contact us.

    BRAND PARTNERSHIPS

    We are the brand ambassador for a number of brands, and other several top world class brands, and are always open to discussing new partnerships. For brand partnership inquiries, please contact us.
